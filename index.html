Maime Content Gen

A personal macOS app that turns a music track into a vertical music video.

Maime Content Gen is a single-user desktop tool. It transcribes a song's lyrics locally, plans a storyboard, generates video clips through the creator's own video generation provider account, and assembles the final vertical video with the original audio and synced captions.

The app can optionally publish the finished video to the creator's own YouTube and TikTok accounts, using each account's official API and the creator's own authorization.
